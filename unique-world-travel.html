Good Questions

You Asked. Here's the Answer.

Can I make payments?

Yes — payment plans are available. After your $500 deposit is secured, contact Unique directly to set up a payment schedule that works for you. Final payment is due April 10, 2027.

Do I have to get travel insurance?

Travel insurance is not included but is strongly recommended. This is an international trip — insurance protects your investment if something unexpected comes up before or during the cruise.

What's the refund policy?

All deposits are non-refundable. Once your $500 deposit is paid and your cabin is confirmed, that secures your spot. Review Royal Caribbean's cancellation policy for details related to final payments.

Are gratuities included?

Gratuities are not included in your cabin price. Royal Caribbean charges a daily gratuity per guest that is automatically added to your onboard account. Contact Unique for the most current daily rate.
